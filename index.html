Virtural Tracing
Geolocation in general refers to the identification of the real geographical area of an electronic device, such as a cell phone, IP addresses, WiFi, and MAC addresses. Now that being said that does not mean an IP address can be traced directly to a house. Geolocation particularly for IP addresses is not an exact science. Unlike cell phones that can be traced via their GPS coordinates or cell tower triangulation, IP addresses use a common database of address locations maintained by different companies.
